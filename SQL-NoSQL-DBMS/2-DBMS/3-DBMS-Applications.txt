# Applications of DBMS:
  • Banking: Manages accounts and transactions (We use DBMS to store information of customer details, accounts, loans, banking transactions, credit card transactions).
  • E-commerce: Tracks products, orders, and customers.
  • Healthcare: Stores patient records and diagnoses.
  • Education: Handles student grades and schedules (We use DBMS to store students and staff information).
  • Social Media: Manages user profiles and interactions.
  • Data Science: Supports analytics and predictions.
  
  • Enterprise: We use DBMS to store information of sales, accounting, human resources, Manufacturing, online retailers.
  • Airlines: We use DBMS to store information of reservations and schedules.
